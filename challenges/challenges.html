Do people speak English in Sofia? The Bulgarian language uses the Cyrillic alphabet, which might be confusing at first. Yet most signs around the city and in public transport appear both in Bulgarian and English. Many people, especially the younger generation, speak good English and can help you get around.
35% of the country's population claim to possess a workable knowledge of this language. English is the second most common foreign language in Bulgaria. Italian and Spanish and French are the other popular foreign languages spoken in the country.

Bulgaria has a warm temperate climate with idyllic summers and short cool winters. The best time to visit Bulgaria is between June and August. The hottest month of the year is August with an average daily maximum of 29 C and an average low of 19 C.
